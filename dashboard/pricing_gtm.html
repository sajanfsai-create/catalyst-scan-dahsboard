Sales Playbook

Acquisition Strategies

4 proven plays to generate pipeline and close deals across all segments

1

Product-Led Free Trial

Offer 5 free scans on signup. IT managers run it on their own laptop — the depth and speed of data instantly proves the value. No sales call needed for initial hook.

✉️ Email template: "See your own hardware report in 3 seconds"
2

Direct MSP Outreach (LinkedIn + Email)

Target MSP owners, vCIOs, and System Integrators. Highlight the multi-tenant dashboard and the ability to generate branded .exe per client.

📧 Subject: "Onboard new IT clients in 3 minutes — no RMM needed"
3

Compliance Readiness Campaigns

Target CFOs, compliance officers, and IT directors at universities and SMEs. Position CatalystScan as the fastest path to an "Asset Register" — step #1 for any ISO, AICTE, or E-Waste compliance.

🎓 Webinar: "AICTE IT Audit in 30 Minutes — Live Demo"
4

Partner Referral Program

Incentivize existing partners to refer other consultants. Offer 50 bonus scans per successful referral. Create a network effect where partners bring in more partners.

🤝 "Refer an IT agency → Get 50 free scans"
Execution

90-Day Go-To-Market Timeline

Week 1-2

Technical Readiness

Integrate Razorpay/Stripe, implement scan credit deduction, enable self-service purchase flow. Create branded 1-pager PDF marketing asset showing Multi-Org Dashboard screenshots.

Week 3-4

Seed Partners

Onboard 5-10 pilot MSP partners from local networks. Provide white-glove setup assistance. Collect testimonials and usage data. Target regional IT hardware vendors in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Maharashtra.

Week 5-8

Scale Outbound

Launch LinkedIn ads targeting MSP owners and IT directors. Run the "AICTE Compliance" webinar series. Begin cold email campaigns (3-email sequences) to university registrars and IT procurement heads.

Week 9-12

Optimize & Expand

Analyze conversion data, optimize pricing if needed. Launch the partner referral program. Expand to North India market. Target government e-governance tenders and state university centralized procurement.
